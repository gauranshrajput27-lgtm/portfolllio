1. Product Summary
A premium, conversion-optimized single-page portfolio website for Gauransh Lodhi Rajput—a video editor, motion graphics designer, and web developer. The site acts as a client acquisition tool, routing visitors to WhatsApp and Email. The design is heavily focused on a bright, minimalist aesthetic with massive breathing room, zero gradients, and a highly structured editorial layout.

2. Design System & Visual Identity
The visual language relies entirely on flat colors, stark contrast, and generous whitespace to create a high-end feel. Strict rule: No gradients are to be used anywhere on the site.

2.1 Color Palette
Primary Background: #E8E4DC (Warm, sophisticated off-white/cream).  
TXT

Typography & Elements: #0A0A0F (Deep midnight slate for maximum contrast and readability).  
TXT

2.2 Background Texture
Subtle Overlay: Implement a very low-opacity fine grain/noise layer or ultra-thin minimal grid lines over the #E8E4DC background. This adds physical depth and a premium "print magazine" feel without creating visual clutter.

2.3 Typography
Display / Headlines: Bold, high-impact fonts (e.g., Against, Streamster, or Highrise) used sparingly and with extensive surrounding whitespace.  
TXT

Body Font: Highly legible sans-serif (e.g., Inter or DM Sans) rendered in the dark #0A0A0F accent color.  
TXT
